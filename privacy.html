Overview

The Daily Edge is designed as a bring-your-own-provider analytics app. Users may enter their own API keys, broker credentials, watchlists, preferences, and local account information. The first public launch keeps these credentials on the user's device, giving you complete control over your sensitive data.

Data Stored On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never sent to The Daily Edge servers:

  • Local app account records and optional two-factor authentication setup
  • User-supplied provider API keys for market data sources
  • User-supplied broker or execution credentials for trading platforms
  • Custom watchlists, favorites, preferences, and app settings
  • Cached market scans and paper-trading results

Data Sent To Providers

When you enter a provider key and refresh market data, the app may send requests directly to that provider's API. Supported providers include:

  • The Odds API for sports odds and betting data
  • Alpha Vantage and Twelve Data for stock market data
  • OANDA and Forex providers for currency trading
  • Coinbase and Robinhood Crypto for cryptocurrency data
  • Polymarket and other decentralized exchanges
  • Brave Search, AI providers, and other data sources

Each provider handles your data under their own privacy policy and terms of service. The Daily Edge does not control how third-party providers process your information.

What We Don't Collect At Launch

For your security and privacy, The Daily Edge explicitly does NOT collect:

  • Daily Edge-owned server copies of your provider API keys
  • Daily Edge-owned server copies of your broker private keys
  • Payment card numbers (Apple App Store handles all billing)
  • Sportsbook login credentials
  • Real-money sports bets or transaction history

Account And Data Deletion

You maintain full control over your data. To delete all local app data:

  • Open The Daily Edge app and go to Settings
  • Select "Reset App Data" to completely wipe your device
  • This permanently removes your API keys, credentials, accounts, preferences, cached scans, watchlists, and all other saved app data

Since The Daily Edge doesn't store copies of your data on our servers, there is nothing to delete remotely.

Tracking & Analytics

The first public launch of The Daily Edge does not use third-party tracking services for advertising or analytics purposes. If we add tracking in future updates, this privacy policy and App Store privacy answers will be updated first, and you will be notified.

Contact & Support

For privacy-related questions or support, use the App Store support link for The Daily Edge or visit our Support page. Do not send API keys or credentials to support—we cannot access your local device data.
