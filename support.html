Support & Help Center

Find answers, troubleshooting guides, and important information about The Daily Edge.

Welcome to The Daily Edge support center. This page provides guidance for common issues, troubleshooting steps, and important information about how The Daily Edge works. For issues not covered here, please contact us through the App Store support link.

Before Contacting Support

Please review these steps before reaching out to our support team:

  • Confirm your provider API key is active and valid with the provider
  • Check provider quota limits before refreshing sports odds or market data
  • Verify your internet connection is stable and fast
  • Try restarting the app to refresh all data connections
  • Use Settings → Reset App Data if you need to clear local credentials and cached app data
  • Use Membership → Restore Purchases if a subscription does not appear active

Credential & Security Safety

IMPORTANT: Never send the following information to support or post it online:

  • API keys for any market data provider
  • Broker private keys or authentication tokens
  • Seed phrases or cryptocurrency wallet information
  • Account passwords or login credentials
  • Live trading credentials or account numbers
  • Two-factor authentication codes

The Daily Edge follows a bring-your-own-provider model with all credentials stored locally on your device. We never have access to these details, so there's no way we can help recover lost credentials—only your provider can.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my app data?

Open Settings in The Daily Edge app and select "Reset App Data." This completely removes all local data including API keys, credentials, watchlists, and settings.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Membership → Restore Purchases. Your subscription should appear within moments. If issues persist, contact App Store support.

Where is my data stored?

All your data (API keys, credentials, watchlists) is stored locally on your device only. We never store copies of this information on our servers.

Can you recover my lost API key?

No. Since we don't store copies of your keys, only your provider can help. Contact them directly to regenerate or recover your key.

Why is my market data not updating?

Check that your API key is active with the provider, you haven't exceeded quota limits, and your internet connection is stable. Try restarting the app.

Does The Daily Edge accept live bets?

No. The Daily Edge is a bring-your-own-provider analytics and paper-trading platform. It does not accept or execute real-money bets or trades.

Does The Daily Edge hold my funds or crypto?

No. The Daily Edge does not custody, store, or hold any funds, cryptocurrencies, or assets. All data stays on your device.

Are my market decisions guaranteed?

No. The Daily Edge does not guarantee profits, wins, returns, or market data accuracy. All investment and trading decisions are your responsibility.

Contact Support

For issues not covered in this support center, please reach out to us through the App Store support link for The Daily Edge. When contacting support:

  • Describe the issue in detail with step-by-step reproduction steps
  • Include your device type, OS version, and app version
