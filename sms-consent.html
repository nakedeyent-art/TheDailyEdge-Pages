SMS Consent

Last updated: June 22, 2026

The Daily Edge sends SMS only to users who explicitly opt in. SMS alerts are optional and are not required to use the app.

How Users Opt In

  • A user opens The Daily Edge app and enters their own phone number in account setup or Settings.
  • The user enables optional SMS alerts with an in-app consent control.
  • The app explains that message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, and users may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
  • Consent is stored with the user's account preferences and can be disabled in Settings at any time.

Message Types

Opted-in users may receive account notices, beta testing updates, app status alerts, daily pick reminders, selected market or sports alerts, and whale news alerts.

Consent Language

Example in-app language:

By enabling SMS alerts, I agree to receive optional The Daily Edge text messages including account updates, app status alerts, beta/testing updates, daily pick reminders, selected market/sports alerts, and whale news alerts. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

Opt Out And Help

  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for help.
  • Users may also disable SMS alerts in the app Settings screen.
