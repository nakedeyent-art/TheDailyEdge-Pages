## AUDIO DESIGN

### Music
- **0:00-0:15:** Ambient, beautiful. Bee hum beneath. Establishes honey as peaceful/foundational.
- **0:15-0:30:** Music darkens slightly. Minor key. Tension enters.
- **0:30-0:45:** Rapid montage. Staccato sounds. Different locations, different textures.
- **0:45-0:55:** Music builds. Orchestral elements. Smoke and mystery. Unseen forces.
- **0:55-1:00:** Crescendo into silence. Impact.

### Sound Design
- **Bee hum:** Persistent until midway. Reminds audience of what's at stake.
- **Papers/contracts:** Sharp, precise. Words matter.
- **Radio static:** Modern intrusion. Federal investigation.
- **Sacred bells:** Ethereal, ominous. Hidden powers.
- **Island breathing:** Metaphorical sound of system tensing.

### Voice-Over Delivery
- **Cassius:** Calm, elegant, patient. No rush. Decades-thinking.
- **Rootmane:** Slow, authoritative, weighted. Moral authority.
- **Honeylaw:** Precise, sharp. Legal weapon.
- **Goldcomb:** Ancient wisdom. Protective.
- **Stoneclaw:** Frustrated hardliner. Impatient.
- **Talon:** Abrasive, sharp, confused. Trying to see pattern.

---

## VISUAL STYLE NOTES

### Color Palette
- **Forest scenes:** Greens, golds, warm light. Life.
- **Lower Mountain:** Cool grays, industrial. Clean, sterile.
- **Montage:** Rapid cuts. Different lighting for each territory.
- **Shrine scene:** Ethereal, mysterious. Soft light.
- **Island overview:** Night lights. System visible.

### Camera Movement
- **Early scenes:** Static or slow push in. Contemplative.
- **Montage:** Quick cuts, dynamic angles. Urgency.
- **Shrine scene:** Slow pull back. Reveal scope.
- **Island scene:** Sweeping aerial view. System perspective.

### Visual Effects
- **Honey:** Golden light, glow. Precious.
- **Smoke:** Volumetric smoke from mountain. Transition effect.
- **Map animations:** Red lines showing Gorilla reach. Clean, strategic.
- **Title card:** Smoke + title integration. Natural, not cheesy.
