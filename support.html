The Daily Edge Support

Use this page for launch support, troubleshooting, and App Review reference.

Before You Contact Support

  • Confirm your provider API key is active with the provider.
  • Check provider quota limits before refreshing sports odds or market data.
  • Use Settings > Reset App Data if you need to clear local credentials and cached app data.
  • Use Membership > Restore Purchases if a subscription does not appear active.

Credential Safety

Do not send API keys, broker private keys, seed phrases, account passwords, or live trading credentials to support. The Daily Edge launch model is bring-your-own-provider and device-local.

Important Boundaries

  • The Daily Edge does not accept sports bets.
  • The Daily Edge does not custody funds or crypto.
  • The Daily Edge does not guarantee profit, wins, returns, or accuracy.
  • All market decisions remain the user's responsibility.
