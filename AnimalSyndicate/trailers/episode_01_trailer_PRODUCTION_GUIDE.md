- **Format:** ProRes 422 HQ (.mov) or H.264 (.mp4)
- **Codec:** Apple ProRes 422 HQ recommended for editing flexibility

- **Audio:** 48 kHz, 16-bit stereo WAV files (separate from video)

**Composer Brief:**
- Tone: Dark, sophisticated, prestige drama. Think The Sopranos opening + Game of Thrones political intrigue
- Instruments: Orchestral (strings, woodwinds) + electronic elements (subtle, modern)
- Pacing: Slow build from peaceful to ominous

**Timeline:**
- **0:00-0:05:** Ambient, beautiful, gentle. Bee hum as musical element. (Gentle strings, nature sounds, bee hum)
- **0:05-0:15:** Music darkens, minor key introduced. Tension rising. (Strings become minor, subtle low strings, cello foundation)
- **0:15-0:30:** Staccato rhythmic elements, but not action-oriented. Strategic tension. (Pizzicato strings, subtle percussion, building intensity)
- **0:30-0:45:** Rapid montage music—staccato, different textures for each location. (Varied instrumentation, rhythmic driving, different motifs per territory)

- **0:55-1:00:** Crescendo reaches peak, then abrupt silence at title card (1 second of silence for dramatic impact)
